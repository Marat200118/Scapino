Kiss the Darkness

By Xingxing Gong

Meaning of life

What is the meaning of life? What would have happened if your parents have decided not to give birth to you? As a product of an accident, of a lucky coincidence, of a violation of the law, Xingxing is looking for an answer in her one-act performance “Kiss the Darkness” as a part of Origin topic by Scapino.
