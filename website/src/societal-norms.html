Kiss the Darkness

Surveillance camera

It’s always convenient to present as a happy person. What if you don’t feel this way? Would you feel comfortable to express your concerns? In era of toxic optimism and propaganda of happy feelings, Xingxing wants to confront your actual emotions. Through her dance in “Kiss the Darkness” she portrays vulnerability, being unstable, but honest.
